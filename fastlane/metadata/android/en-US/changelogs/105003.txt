1.5.0 (Release)

• Multi-page scanning and export (stable)
  - Add multiple pages from camera or "Completed scans"
  - Filmstrip with thumbnails; tap to preview a page
  - Drag-and-drop to reorder, remove single pages, or "Clear all"
  - Multi-page PDF with optional per-page OCR layer where available
  - JPEG export: single image or multi-page as ZIP (page_001.jpg, …)
  - TXT export combines OCR text in page order

• New "Completed scans" picker
  - Browse previously saved scans; multi-select to add
  - Long-press to remove entries from the registry

• Inline OCR status messages and UX polish
  - Improved dark mode for dialogs, better accessibility labels, and small bug fixes
